Our Story

The Beginning

Born from a Passion for Coffee

Lumina Cafe was born from a deep love of freshly roasted coffee and the belief that a truly great cup can transform an ordinary moment into a lasting memory.

We opened our doors in 2020 with the intention of creating a space where everyone feels at home, while enjoying premium coffee selected with the utmost care.

  • Organic beans sourced from Fair Trade-certified partner farms
  • Expert baristas trained at the World Barista Championship
  • An atmosphere designed for relaxation — ideal for both work and leisure
  • Supporting Thai coffee farming communities through our Bean to Cup programme
